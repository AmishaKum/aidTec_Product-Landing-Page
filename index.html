VARIETIES

Amber

This red, medium-sized fruit becomes fully ripe in mid-October. It is mostly grown in Shopian and Kulgam.

American Trel

American Trel is very crispy and sweet is taste. The Skin of the fruit is White greenish and sweet.

Red Delicious

Red Delicious is a medium-sized apple, with a tall conical shape. The dark and intense crimson colour makes it the quintessential red apple,

Maharaej

A large apple with red and green color. It tastes a bit sour but sweetens with time and is available by late October.

Hazratbael

Hazratbali Apple in medium size with rounds to slightly conical in shape. It has red striped skin with white juicy and sweet flesh.

Golden Delicious

A variety with comparatively longer shelf life, it is crispy, juicy and has thick greenish-white flesh which turns golden upon ripening. It is available till January.
